EATING Disorder

Eating Disorders Includes anorexia nervosa (severe food restriction), bulimia nervosa (binge-eating followed by purging), and binge-eating disorder (recurring episodes of overeating).Anorexia nervosa involves an intense fear of gaining weight, leading to severe food restriction and low body weight. Bulimia nervosa includes cycles of binge-eating followed by compensatory behaviors like vomiting or excessive exercise. Binge-eating disorder involves recurrent episodes of overeating without compensatory behaviors.:* Anorexia nervosa involves severe food restriction and distorted body image. Bulimia nervosa includes binge-eating followed by purging behaviors. Binge-eating disorder involves frequent overeating.
